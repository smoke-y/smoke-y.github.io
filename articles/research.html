research

My ideas on how to approach research. Will refine over time.

Most of these ideas are stolen from here. Watch it. Quick summary:

  • Attract the Muses by keeping your mind in a "receiving" state(clear mind, keeping track of your attention, etc)
  • Find physical activities that clear your mind, ready to receive ideas from the subconscious
  • Chasing "Success" kills deep work

Maintaining a record. Any ablation study, blog, training run, observation, etc. should be recorded in a systematic way. I use logseq to maintain a log of experiments I run, ablation study, or blogs I found interesting.

Huge emphasis on creating an environment that lets you come up with ideas and quickly perform experiments. For the former, long and intense exposure, low cortisol environment, etc. For the latter, deep understanding of your tools, codex/claude code, etc.
